Distribution System

Boyko Surlev, Nikolaj Desting & Peter Tomaščík
BoNiPe

Our website represents a 'Distribution system' where an administrator
can offer products and a customer can buy them.

An admin can login so that he/she can control the products, which can be sold to customer, the actual orders and payments
from the customers and full control over their accounts. A customer can login so that she/he can order different products,
control his/her orders or payments methods and also have full control over his/her account.

However, our program is small - we should admit that there is load of work which should be done in order to have a fully
working distribution system. We believe that this is good enough to what we have learnt during our last semester
